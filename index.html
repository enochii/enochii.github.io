Short Bio

 I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Lian Li. Prior to this, I obtained my Bachelor's degree at Tongji University.

I am broadly interested in topics related to programming language and software engineering, with a focus on techniques that enhance software security, reliability, and performance. Particularly, I enjoy working on program analysis problems from vairous perspectives such as graph/database theory and formal languages. I am deeply amazed by the connection of techniques across different research communities.

:-) Feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in my work.
